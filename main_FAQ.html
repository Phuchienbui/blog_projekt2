FAQ

What’s the story behind my travel blog?

It all started with a one-way ticket and a backpack that was definitely too heavy. I realized that life is too short to stay in one place, so I traded my office desk for mountain peaks and city sunsets. This blog isn’t just about the destinations; it’s about the lessons learned when things go hilariously wrong and the magic found in the middle of nowhere.

How do I plan, document, and share my travels?

The "Digital Journal": Use an app like Notion to jot down quick sensory details: what did the market smell like? What was the exact price of that street food? These details make your writing authentic.
